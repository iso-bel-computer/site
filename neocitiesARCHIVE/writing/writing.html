THUS THE FEMALE DOUBLE LIFE, TO ESCORT YOUR IMAGE, (AND TO BE ESCORTED BY YOUR IMAGE), MAKES GENERAL (AND IS MADE GENERAL).

ESCAPE VELOCITY EJECTS YOU FROM THE ORBIT OF YOUR LOVER. SPRINTING INTO THE DARK, YOU SING THE LAST WORDS YOU SPOKE TO HIM, AND TRACE HIS FADING TRANSPONDER. RETURNING A CENTURY LATER, YOUR MANTRA IS WARPED BY CORRUPTION AND STELLAR RADIATION. SEA FOAM AND PHLEGM EXPLODES FROM YOUR MOUTH AND ILLUMINATES YOUR RELUCTANT SLINGSHOT. JETTISONED BACKWASH RE-ENTERS AN EMPTY STAGE.

YOU DON'T THINK ABOUT WHERE YOU'RE GOING IN YOUR HOMETOWN. COMMUTERS BEAT THEIR RAT LINES TO WORK AND VICE. THIS FUGUE MARCH CLEARS LAND IN THE MIND FOR TRADE, SEX AND SLEEP. MUNDANE DISRUPTION IS EXPECTED AND REQUIRED. ONLY VIOLENT INTERLOPERS REMIND YOU TO BREATHE.

YOU STOP DEAD AND IMAGINE YOURSELF IN A PAINTING. BEFORE YOUR POISED STANCE, THE RIVERS LATEST TENANTS PRESS DOWN ON THE SILT. BANK PIGEONS DIVE AND MUDLARK VICARIOUSLY. HIGH BEAMS SWEEP THE TRACKS AND PICK YOU OUT. SOON YOU WILL BE HUNG IN A GALLERY, YOUR AURA SINGULAR AND UNIQUE. CROWDS ADMIRE YOUR EMPTY CUP BEHIND REINFORCED GLASS.
